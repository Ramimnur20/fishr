# FISHR
🎣 Fish, earn, and compete! Economy bot with Dynamic Economy, Ai Chatbot and Fishing!

FishR is your go-to Discord economy bot with a playful twist. Catch fish, earn credits, level up your score, and grab rare boosts from the shop.
🎣 Fishing Minigame: Cast your line, catch fish, and try to snag rare boosts like Fish Madness for 4x fish!
💰 Dynamic Economy: Sell your fish for credits at a rate that changes every 5 minutes. Watch the market, maximize your gains, and build your wealth.
🏦 Bank & Transactions: Deposit, withdraw, or attempt to steal from friends—strategy and luck combine!
🎯 Score Farm: Solve fast math questions to earn points, but watch out for distractions.
💎 Treasure Chests: Wild chests appear in active chats, giving you coins and fish if you’re quick enough.
FishR is designed to be fast, fun, and full of surprises. Perfect for communities who love casual games, friendly competition, and a bit of chaos.
